## Cross-Book Utility Rules (Implementation)
1. If a supporting character appears in Bn, show one consequence from B(n-1).
2. At least one supporting actor per book must change factional stance for non-sentimental reasons.
3. No antagonist exits without either replacement pressure or doctrine residue.
4. Evidence releases require three-link validation chain: source, custody, public trust broker.
5. Logistics beats must visibly constrain tactical options in every major arc block.
